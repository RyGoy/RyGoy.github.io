Hanged is most appropriate for official executions <he was to be hanged, cut down whilst still aliveand his bowels torn out Louis Allen> but hung is also used <gave orders that she should be hung Peter Quennell>. Hung is more appropriate for less formal hangings <by morning Ill be hung in effigy Ronald Reagan>.
